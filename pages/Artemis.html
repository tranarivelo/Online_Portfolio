Projet Artemis

Unreal Engine

Personal project in duo on UE5. A single-player FPS divided into multiple levels, allowing the player to follow a linear narrative.
During this project, we will need to implement tools that enable the player to move and interact smoothly with their environment, through complex movement mechanics and a cover system. We will also include ballistic features for our weapons. We want all bullets to be physical projectiles that react dynamically with the environment and enemies:
-Ricochets
-Penetration
-Sway
The level design should allow the player to approach most situations as they wish, whether it’s charging in head-on, eliminating enemies stealthily, or even choosing not to interact with them at all.
