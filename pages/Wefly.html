WeFly

Unreal Engine

Using Unreal Engine, I am currently developing a sandbox flight simulator based on advanced physics. This project includes features such as vector lift, stall behavior, angle of attack, and air density. These elements allow for a realistic reproduction of flight conditions and aircraft behavior, providing an immersive simulation experience that faithfully reflects real aerodynamic principles.
