Golberg Machine VR

Unity

This virtual reality (VR) project recreates a Rube Goldberg machine — a complex device designed to perform a simple task in an exaggerated and chaotic way. Players can interact with various mechanisms such as ramps, gears, pulleys, and trigger objects to observe or design their own chain reactions. Thanks to VR, the experience offers an engaging and playful perspective, allowing users to manipulate elements in real time while exploring physics and engineering principles in a fun and interactive environment.
