League Of Rocket

Unity

Our ongoing project in Unity was an exciting exploration of procedural generation and physics in game development. Through a series of challenges, we created dynamic environments and interactive experiences using procedural generation algorithms. At the same time, we deepened our understanding of game physics by applying realistic principles to make interactions with the environment as authentic as possible. This project allowed us to push our creative boundaries while gaining essential skills for game development in Unity.
